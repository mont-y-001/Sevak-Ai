About SevakAI

SevakAI is an AI-powered volunteer coordination portal for the Google Solution Challenge 2026. It enables NGO field coordinators to submit weekly volunteer needs via automated AI phone calls in Hindi and regional languages — no smartphone, app, or internet required.

Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash · Google Cloud Speech-to-Text · Firebase · Flutter · Exotel
