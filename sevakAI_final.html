🙋 Volunteer Portal

Browse open volunteer opportunities near you, register your skills, and accept tasks — all in one place. Get notified via WhatsApp when NGOs need you.

How Volunteers Get Notified

You never have to check the portal daily — SevakAI comes to you.

💬
WhatsApp Notification System

When an NGO submits a need and your skills + pincode match, SevakAI sends you a WhatsApp message instantly. You reply YES to accept or NO to decline — no app needed. If you decline, the next matched volunteer is automatically notified.

1

Register Once

Sign up with your mobile number, skills (food/health/education), home pincode, and availability. OTP verified.

2

AI Matches You

Gemini AI ranks you based on skill match, pincode proximity, and availability every time an NGO submits a need.

3

Get WhatsApp Alert

You receive a WhatsApp message with task details — org name, location, date, what's needed. No app install required.

4

Accept in One Tap

Reply YES to confirm. Reply NO to pass — the next volunteer is auto-notified. Urgency-5 tasks alert multiple volunteers at once.

5

Complete & Log

After the task, submit a 2-tap SMS feedback. Your hours and impact are logged to your volunteer profile automatically.

6

Earn Certificate

Accumulate hours to earn a verified digital certificate — usable for college applications and corporate CSR records.
